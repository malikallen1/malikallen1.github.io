Technology Advancements in a Decade from Now

When I asked my mother about what advancements in the technology of computers does she expect to see in a decade from now, she responded by stating that she would like to see future computers being built upon the foundation of today’s computers. She states that the technology inside computers have come a long way since the late 1970’s. She mentions that there could be no physical machines as we know them, but instead advanced computing systems could fit on people’s bodies like a piece of jewelry. She further elaborates by mentioning that people will continue to control the computers and that it is something that isn’t likely to change in what lies ahead.

When I asked my aunt about what advancements in the technology of computers does she expect to see in a decade from now, she answers by stating that the future of computers will be so much more advanced than today’s computers as programmers learn more and more about the technology. She mentions that there will exponential improvements of computer processing power, voice recognition, image recognition, and more in-depth learning. She states that everything is currently computer operated and that she is expecting more advancement in a decade from now. She also states that there could be no physical machines as we know them.

When I asked my cousin about what advancements in the technology of computers does she expect to see in a decade from now, she responded by stating that she expects technology to evolve to another level. She stated that she expects upgraded features to be implemented on computers and laptops, such as sound quality and graphics on websites and other software. She also expects hands-free typing or other accommodations that help disabled or impaired people to be more common place in the future. To summarize, she expects technology in computers to advance to the point where there are impressive upgrades and accessibility options.
