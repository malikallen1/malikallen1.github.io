When I asked my mother about her thoughts on computers and other devices, she responded stating that she found computers to be “amazing”. She states that the main reason that she believed computers are amazing is because they are used to access the internet for finding any kind of information. She states that she found so much information on her college assignments when she attended DeVry for her homework. She adds on to that mentioning that if she didn’t have a computer when she was a student, it would’ve been more stressful for her. She concluded her response by stating that “using computers helps everyone”.

When I asked my aunt about her thoughts on computers and other devices, she answered by stating that her main thought is that computers, cell phones, iPads, and other devices are the way of the future. She also states that technology has taken over the world for the good with the intent to make everyone’s daily life so much easier to maneuver. She mentions that computers are used to access the internet for finding any kind of information, which she finds “amazing”. She concludes the interview by mentioning that if she had any sort of question, she could her computer to access the internet in order to find the answer to anything.

When I asked my cousin about her thought on computers and other devices, she responded by mentioning that cell phones, tablets and other devices have helped generations function in society. She also mentions that phones, tablets and other devices have negatively impacted people today. She states that the average person has a ridiculous amount of screen time which affects the time that should be spent outdoors and socializing with family and friends. She expands on that thought further by mentioning that you would find people glued to their phones or other devices today at a family event and discussing social media gossip and trending topics.
